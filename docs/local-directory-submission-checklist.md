## Submission Best Practices

### 1. NAP Consistency (Critical)

**Always Use:**
- **Exact same** business name
- **Exact same** address format
- **Exact same** phone number format

**Example:**
✅ **Correct:** こども学園 / Kodomo Gakuen
❌ **Incorrect:** Kodomo Gakuen (missing Japanese name)

✅ **Correct:** 2-409 Narahashi, Higashiyamato, Tokyo 207-0031
❌ **Incorrect:** 2-409-0 Narahashi, Higashiyamato-shi, Tokyo 207-0031

### 2. Descriptions

**Keep Consistent Core Information:**
- Business type (preschool/kindergarten)
- Age range (0-5 years)
- Bilingual education
- Location (Higashi Yamato)
- Special features (Yokota Air Base support)

**Adapt Length to Platform:**
- Short (100 chars): Twitter bio, directory summaries
- Medium (300 chars): Google Business Profile
- Long (500+ chars): Website-style descriptions

### 3. Categories

**Primary Categories (Priority Order):**
1. Preschool (プレスクール)
2. Kindergarten (幼稚園)
3. Day care center (保育園)
4. Childcare agency (子育て支援)

**Select ALL applicable categories** on platforms that allow multiple selections.

### 4. Photos

**Upload Consistent Photos Across Platforms:**
- Logo (square, 500x500px minimum)
- Cover photo (1200x630px for social media)
- 5-10 exterior/interior photos
- Activities photos (blur children's faces or obtain consent)

**Photo Naming:**
- kodomo-gakuen-logo.png
- kodomo-gakuen-exterior.jpg
- kodomo-gakuen-classroom-1.jpg

### 5. Links

**Always Include:**
- Website URL: https://www.kodomogakuen.com
- Google Maps link (from Google Business Profile)

**Optional:**
- Social media links (Twitter, Instagram)
- LINE Official Account (if created)

### 6. Verification

**Prioritize Verification:**
- Verified listings rank higher
- Verified = trusted by users
- Most platforms offer free verification

**Methods:**
- Postcard (most common)
- Phone call (automated)
- Email
- Document upload (business license)
