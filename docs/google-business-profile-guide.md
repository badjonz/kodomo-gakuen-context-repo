## Photos and Videos

### Photo Requirements and Best Practices

**Minimum Photos:** 3
**Recommended:** 20-30 high-quality photos
**Optimal:** Update monthly with new photos

### Photo Categories

#### 1. Exterior Photos (Required)
- **Storefront:** Building exterior, entrance, signage
- **Parking:** Show available parking
- **Accessibility:** Wheelchair ramps, entrance
- **Recommended:** 3-5 photos

#### 2. Interior Photos (Highly Recommended)
- **Classrooms:** Clean, bright, organized spaces
- **Play areas:** Indoor play equipment, reading corners
- **Facilities:** Bathrooms, kitchen (if shown to parents), nap areas
- **Recommended:** 10-15 photos

**Photo Tips:**
- Good lighting (natural light preferred)
- No children's faces without parent consent (privacy)
- Show clean, organized spaces
- Include bilingual materials/posters visible

#### 3. Team Photos (Recommended)
- **Staff:** Teachers and caregivers (with consent)
- **Team activities:** Staff training, meetings
- **Recommended:** 3-5 photos

**Privacy Note:** Obtain written permission before posting staff photos.

#### 4. Activity Photos
- **Daily activities:** Arts & crafts, reading time, outdoor play
- **Special events:** Festivals, seasonal activities, field trips
- **Meals:** Nutritious lunch/snack offerings
- **Recommended:** 15-20 photos, rotate seasonally

**Child Privacy:**
- Blur children's faces or photograph from behind
- OR obtain signed photo consent forms from all parents
- Highlight activities, not individual children

#### 5. Identity Photos
- **Logo:** High-resolution school logo
- **Cover photo:** Most important - appears at top of profile
  - **Size:** 1024x576px (16:9 ratio)
  - **Content:** School exterior or engaging classroom scene
  - **Text:** Minimal or none (against guidelines to add promotional text)

### Video Content

**Google Business Profile Supports:**
- Video uploads up to 30 seconds (via Google Posts)
- YouTube video links (in posts)

**Video Ideas:**
- Virtual tour of facilities
- Welcome message from director/teachers
- Day-in-the-life montage
- Seasonal event highlights
- Parent testimonials (with permission)

**Video Best Practices:**
- Keep under 1 minute for engagement
- Add subtitles (bilingual: Japanese/English)
- Good audio quality (parent testimonials)
- Professional editing (stable footage, good lighting)

### Photo Upload Tips

1. **Rename files before uploading:**
   - Good: `kodomo-gakuen-classroom-1.jpg`
   - Bad: `IMG_1234.jpg`

2. **Image Quality:**
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Size: At least 720px wide, 720px tall
   - Resolution: 72 DPI minimum
   - File size: Under 5MB

3. **Upload Frequency:**
   - New photos monthly (shows active business)
   - Seasonal updates (cherry blossoms in spring, etc.)
   - Event photos within 24 hours of event

## Reviews Management

### Why Reviews Matter

- **Trust:** 88% of parents trust online reviews as much as personal recommendations
- **Rankings:** Review quantity and quality impact local search rankings
- **Conversion:** More reviews = higher click-through and enrollment rates

### Encouraging Reviews

**Ethical Methods:**
1. **After Enrollment:** Send follow-up email thanking parents, gently requesting review
2. **At Events:** During open houses, mention "If you enjoyed the tour, we'd appreciate a review"
3. **Email Signature:** Include link to Google review page
4. **QR Code:** Print QR code linking to review page, display at reception

**Direct Review Link:**
```
https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=YOUR_PLACE_ID
```

(Find your Place ID in Google Business Profile dashboard)

**âš ï¸ DO NOT:**

## Messaging Setup

### Enable Messaging

**Location:** Google Business Profile > Messages

**Benefits:**
- Parents can message directly from Google Search/Maps
- Instant communication for quick questions
- Higher engagement than phone calls for some parents
- Can respond via Google Business Profile app

**Setup:**
1. Turn on messaging in dashboard
2. Download **Google Business Profile** app (iOS/Android)
3. Enable push notifications
4. Set response expectations:
   - "We typically reply within a few hours"
   - "We typically reply within a day"

**Response Time Goal:** < 4 hours during business hours
